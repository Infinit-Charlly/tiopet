# TIOPET_PRODUCT_METRICS.md

TioPet — Product Metrics & Growth Indicators

Author: Charlly Acuña  
Purpose: Define the key metrics used to measure the health, growth, and sustainability of the TioPet platform.

---

# 1. Philosophy of Metrics

TioPet should track metrics that answer three fundamental questions:

1. Are users adopting the platform?
2. Are pets being booked regularly?
3. Is the business generating sustainable revenue?

Metrics must remain simple during MVP but scalable for future analytics.

---

# 2. Core Platform Metrics

These metrics determine if the platform is growing.

### Total Users

Number of registered users.

Purpose:

- measure platform adoption

---

### Active Users

Users that performed at least one action during the last 30 days.

Examples of actions:

- creating a booking
- editing a pet profile
- viewing booking status

---

### Total Pets Registered

Total pets registered in the system.

Indicator of platform engagement.

---

### Active Pets

Pets with at least one booking in the last 30 days.

---

# 3. Booking Metrics

These are the most important metrics for TioPet.

### Total Bookings

Total number of reservations created.

---

### Completed Bookings

Bookings successfully completed.

---

### Cancelled Bookings

Bookings cancelled before completion.

---

### Booking Completion Rate

Formula:

completed_bookings / total_bookings

This measures platform reliability.

---

### Average Booking Value (ABV)

Formula:

total_revenue / total_bookings

Measures economic value of each reservation.

---

# 4. Operational Metrics

These help manage the physical operation.

### Location Occupancy Rate

Formula:

active_bookings / location_capacity

Helps determine if a location is underutilized or saturated.

---

### Caregiver Utilization

Percentage of time a caregiver has active bookings.

---

### Average Pet Stay Duration

Average time pets remain under care.

---

# 5. Store Metrics

For the pet store integration.

### Store Conversion Rate

Formula:

store_purchases / bookings

Measures how often users buy products during reservations.

---

### Average Store Basket

Average amount spent on store products.

---

# 6. Customer Experience Metrics

### Customer Rating

Average rating of completed bookings.

Scale:

1–5 stars

---

### Repeat Booking Rate

Formula:

repeat_customers / total_customers

Measures loyalty.

---

# 7. Growth Metrics

### Monthly Growth Rate

Formula:

(new_users_current_month - previous_month_users) / previous_month_users

---

### Referral Rate

Percentage of new users coming from referrals.

---

# 8. Trust Metrics

Because pet care is trust-based.

### Photo Updates per Booking

Number of updates sent to owners.

Higher values increase trust.

---

### Timeline Activity Score

Number of care events recorded per booking.

---

# 9. Security Metrics

Important for operational safety.

### QR Validation Success Rate

Successful check-ins / total check-ins.

---

### Incident Reports

Number of operational issues.

Should remain extremely low.

---

# 10. Dashboard (Future)

Admin dashboard should visualize:

Users
Bookings
Revenue
Occupancy
Ratings

Charts recommended:

- booking growth
- revenue growth
- location occupancy

---

# 11. Early Stage KPI (Most Important)

During MVP, focus on only three metrics:

1. Total bookings
2. Repeat booking rate
3. Average booking value

If these three grow, the business is healthy.
