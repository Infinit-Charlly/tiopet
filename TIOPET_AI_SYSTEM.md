# TIOPET_AI_SYSTEM.md

TioPet — Artificial Intelligence System

Author: Charlly Acuña  
Purpose: Define how Artificial Intelligence can enhance pet safety, monitoring, and customer trust in the TioPet ecosystem.

---

# 1. AI Philosophy

Artificial Intelligence in TioPet should serve three main goals:

1. Improve pet safety
2. Increase owner trust
3. Assist caregivers with monitoring

AI should augment human care, not replace it.

---

# 2. AI Monitoring System

Future TioPet locations may include cameras and audio monitoring.

AI models could analyze:

- barking intensity
- abnormal noise
- aggressive behavior
- pet distress signals
- inactivity anomalies

Possible outcomes:

- alert caregivers
- notify admin
- notify pet owner if necessary

---

# 3. Sound Detection System

AI can analyze audio patterns to detect:

Strong continuous barking  
Possible dog fights  
High stress signals

Example workflow:

Audio input → AI model → classification → alert

---

# 4. Behavior Detection

Using computer vision, AI can detect:

- animals fighting
- animals stuck or trapped
- excessive agitation
- abnormal inactivity

Models could run on edge devices or cloud processing.

---

# 5. Smart Alerts

Alerts should follow priority levels.

Low priority

Pet barking  
Pet restless

Medium priority

Possible fight detected  
Abnormal noise

High priority

Possible injury  
Pet distress

---

# 6. Caregiver Assistance

AI could assist caregivers by:

Suggesting feeding times  
Tracking activity patterns  
Monitoring rest cycles

---

# 7. Pet Activity Insights

Owners could receive:

Daily activity summaries

Example:

Today Luna:

Walked 2 times  
Played 3 sessions  
Slept 4 hours

---

# 8. Emotion & Stress Indicators (Experimental)

Future AI models could estimate:

Stress level  
Energy level  
Mood indicators

This could improve quality of care.

---

# 9. Smart Camera Access

Owners could optionally access cameras.

Security rules:

Access limited to active bookings  
Temporary access tokens  
Session expiration

---

# 10. AI Infrastructure

Possible architecture:

Edge cameras  
Cloud processing  
AI inference APIs

Potential technologies:

TensorFlow Lite  
OpenCV  
AWS Rekognition  
Google Vision AI

---

# 11. Privacy Considerations

AI monitoring must respect privacy.

Rules:

No public camera access  
Limited access for owners  
Secure storage of footage

---

# 12. AI Roadmap

Phase 1

Basic activity logging  
Photo updates

Phase 2

Audio anomaly detection

Phase 3

Computer vision monitoring

Phase 4

Behavior intelligence

---

# 13. Ethical Principles

AI must prioritize:

Animal welfare  
Owner transparency  
Caregiver support

Never replace responsible human supervision.
