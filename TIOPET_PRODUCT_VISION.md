# TIOPET PRODUCT VISION

## Mission

TioPet exists to provide safe, transparent, and loving care for pets while giving peace of mind to their owners.

Our mission is to build a platform that connects pet owners with trusted care, professional facilities, and useful services that improve the well-being of animals.

TioPet is built **for those who have no voice** — our pets.

---

# Core Concept

TioPet begins as a **centralized pet care center platform** and gradually evolves into a **complete ecosystem for pet services**.

The product will grow in stages to ensure stability, operational reliability, and scalable architecture.

The platform is designed with three layers:

1. **Client Experience**
2. **Operational Management**
3. **Intelligent Services and Expansion**

---

# Primary Business Model

The core of TioPet is a **physical or managed pet care center** offering services such as:

- Daycare
- Boarding / Pet hotel
- Transportation
- Grooming
- Socialization
- Exercise
- Feeding supervision
- Health observation

Clients use the app to reserve services and track their pet's activities.

# Pet Care Tracking

A core feature of TioPet is **transparent care tracking**.

During the stay, caretakers log pet activities.

Examples:

- fed
- snack given
- played
- socialized with other pets
- walk
- nap
- grooming
- bath
- brushing
- massage
- medication
- incident report

These logs create a **timeline of the pet's day**.

Owners can view updates in the app.

TioPet includes **caregiver profiles** for internal staff.

Caregivers can:

- see assigned pets
- log care activities
- upload photos
- report incidents
- mark check-in and check-out
- record walks or playtime

Caregivers help create trust and accountability.

---

# QR-based Pet Pickup

For safety, pet pickup can be verified using **QR validation**.

Process:

1. Booking generates pickup authorization.
2. Owner receives QR code.
3. At pickup, staff scans QR.
4. System confirms the correct person retrieves the pet.

Benefits:

- security
- traceability
- accountability

# Payments and Compliance

TioPet must support payment flows compatible with Ecuadorian financial regulations.

Future payment support may include:

- card payments
- transfers
- local payment providers

Financial flows must be compatible with Ecuador's **SRI tax system** and proper invoicing processes.

---

# Future Marketplace Expansion

In the future, TioPet may allow external caregivers to offer services.

These caregivers ("Tíos") could:

- register their home as a care space
- define availability
- define pricing
- accept bookings

However, this introduces additional complexity such as:

- trust and safety
- verification
- insurance
- reputation systems
- location matching

This marketplace model will only be considered after the core platform is stable.

---

# Intelligent Services (AI)

TioPet may later integrate AI capabilities to improve care monitoring.

Possible features include:

- detection of excessive barking
- noise anomaly detection
- possible fight detection
- activity classification
- automated care summaries

AI features will be introduced only after sufficient operational data is available.

---

# Development Philosophy

TioPet follows these principles:

1. Build a stable MVP first
2. Improve operational tools
3. Add transparency features
4. Expand services gradually
5. Introduce intelligence only when supported by real data
