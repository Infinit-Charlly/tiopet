# TIOPET_FRANCHISE_MODEL.md

TioPet — Franchise & Expansion Model

Author: Charlly Acuña  
Purpose: Define the expansion strategy for the TioPet ecosystem across cities and regions.

---

# 1. Vision

TioPet is not only an app.

It is a **pet care ecosystem** composed of:

• Official TioPet locations  
• Independent caregivers  
• Digital booking platform  
• Pet services marketplace  
• Pet product store

The long-term goal is to create the **most trusted pet care network in Latin America.**

---

# 2. Expansion Philosophy

Growth must follow three principles:

1. Trust first
2. Quality of care
3. Technology-driven operations

Every location should follow **standardized operational protocols.**

---

# 3. TioPet Location Types

The ecosystem includes two main types of service providers.

### Official TioPet Centers

Physical locations operating under the TioPet brand.

Services may include:

• daycare  
• hostal  
• grooming  
• training  
• pet socialization areas

Advantages:

• higher capacity  
• full operational control  
• brand experience

---

### Partner Caregivers

Independent caregivers registered on the platform.

Characteristics:

• operate from their homes or small facilities  
• limited capacity  
• flexible schedules

Requirements:

• identity verification  
• home verification  
• safety standards

---

# 4. Franchise Model

Future expansion may include franchise partners.

Franchise benefits:

• use of TioPet brand
• access to booking platform
• operational management tools
• marketing visibility

Franchise responsibilities:

• maintain quality standards
• follow TioPet operational protocols
• use the official platform

---

# 5. Revenue Streams

TioPet generates revenue through multiple channels.

### Booking Commission

Percentage taken from each reservation.

Example:

10%–20% per booking.

---

### Store Sales

Revenue from:

• pet food
• snacks
• toys
• hygiene products

---

### Premium Services

Optional services:

• grooming
• training
• veterinary check
• transportation

---

### Franchise Licensing (future)

Monthly franchise fee or revenue share.
